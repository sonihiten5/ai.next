Why Attend

Designed for developers and technology decision makes, IncubateIND's AI.NXT brought to you by Microsoft and partnered with Nasscom FutureSkills is a FREE virtual workshop series 4 modules of 2.5 hours duration each spread across 2 days.

The session will include an opening note by leading AI experts who will share latest trends and key insights in the field of AI. Each module will be delivered by industry experts and feature a mix of engaging talks, live demos, quiz and hands-on assignments.
For each module you complete, you earn a badge of attendance from Microsoft proving that you have taken your first steps in the exciting field of AI.
